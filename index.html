FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sign a Selfie.

What is Sign a Selfie?
Sign a Selfie is a mobile app that lets you capture selfies with celebrities and have them digitally signed, creating authenticated keepsakes you can keep forever and share with friends.
How much does it cost?
The app is free to download. Some premium signature requests may have a small fee depending on the celebrity, but basic features are completely free.
Is the app available on both iOS and Android?
Yes! Sign a Selfie is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Download it today and start creating signed memories.
How are signatures verified?
Each digital signature goes through our proprietary verification process to ensure authenticity. Every signed selfie comes with a unique certificate of authenticity.
Can I share my signed selfies on social media?
Absolutely! You can share your signed selfies directly to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, and any other platform with just one tap from within the app.
How do I contact support?
You can reach us through the contact form on this page or email us at hello@signaselfie.com. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours.
